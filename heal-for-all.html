For funders & grant partners

Sponsor a clinic — fund a specific, reportable outcome

Most digital-health tools ask funders to underwrite a platform. We ask you to underwrite a clinic — a specific, named facility — and we report back exactly how many scans it ran.

The fundable unit: one sponsored clinic = one year of free ScanGuru access for a verified underserved facility = a defined monthly scan allocation, reported back with real usage data drawn straight from our counter.

Problem

Billions live where there is no radiologist. Scans go unread or wait weeks.

Intervention

An explainable AI second read with a dual-language report, in a browser, free.

Outcome

Faster triage, fewer missed findings, results patients can actually read.

Evidence

Every clinic's usage is counted and reported. University partners validate independently.
