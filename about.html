About

Equal access to diagnostic confidence

ScanGuru is built by Data Dance Inc. to put explainable, multilingual imaging AI within reach of clinics, hospitals, and universities everywhere — not only the best-resourced ones.

Most imaging AI is built for a handful of modalities and a handful of languages. ScanGuru takes the opposite approach: broad modality coverage, explainable findings, and dual-language reports — the patient’s language alongside English — so a clinic in any market can put AI to work without changing how it already operates.

That breadth is the point. It is what lets a university radiology group, a city hospital, and a rural clinic all use the same platform, and it is why our Heal for All program offers free access to verified low-income-country clinics and charity hospitals.

ScanGuru brings those threads together: the discipline of interpreting signals and the engineering rigor to run it at scale — applied to making explainable diagnostic support genuinely accessible across languages, modalities, and budgets. The platform is built lean and clinician-first, with an advisory network of university and clinical partners growing through our pilots.
