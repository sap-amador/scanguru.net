⚙️ Technical Integration

How does ScanGuru integrate with our PACS?
▼
ScanGuru integrates via standard DICOM protocols:

  • DICOM Send: Automatic routing of studies from PACS to ScanGuru
  • DICOM Query/Retrieve: On-demand image retrieval
  • DICOM SR: Structured reporting with results sent back to PACS
  • Worklist Integration: Modality worklist (MWL) support

Certified Compatible PACS:
  • Major vendors: GE, Philips, Siemens, Canon
  • Leading PACS: Sectra, Agfa, Fujifilm, McKesson
  • Cloud PACS: Ambra Health, Life Image, Intelerad

No changes to radiologist workflow - AI results appear directly in your existing viewing environment.
What are the system requirements?
▼
Cloud-Based Deployment (Recommended):
  • Internet bandwidth: 10+ Mbps dedicated
  • DICOM router or gateway appliance (provided if needed)
  • Modern web browser for dashboard access
  • No local server infrastructure required

Hybrid Deployment:
  • On-premise processing for sensitive data
  • Cloud dashboard and analytics
  • Custom security requirements

Network Requirements:
  • Outbound HTTPS (port 443) access
  • DICOM C-STORE, C-FIND, C-MOVE support
  • VPN or direct connect options available
What imaging formats are supported?
▼
Supported Modalities & Formats:

  • CR/DX (Chest X-Ray): Standard DICOM, JPEG compressed
  • CT: Axial, coronal, sagittal reconstructions; thin and thick slice
  • MR: T1, T2, FLAIR, STIR, DWI sequences across all anatomical regions
  • MG (Mammography): Full-field digital mammography, tomosynthesis
  • US (Ultrasound): B-mode, Doppler (pilot phase)
  • PT (PET): FDG-PET/CT fusion studies

All standard DICOM transfer syntaxes supported including compressed formats.

🏥 Clinical Use

How accurate is ScanGuru's AI?
▼
ScanGuru is offered for research use and is not FDA cleared or CE marked. We don’t publish accuracy figures we can’t independently stand behind. Our models are evaluated internally on held-out data with clinician review — internal evaluation is not the same as independent clinical validation, and a qualified clinician reviews and confirms every finding. See our validation approach for how we develop and evaluate models.
Can radiologists override AI findings?
▼
Absolutely. ScanGuru is an assistive tool, not a replacement for radiologist expertise.

Radiologist Authority:
  • Full control over final report
  • Ability to accept, modify, or reject AI findings
  • Option to add findings AI didn't detect
  • Easy override workflow with reason capture

Learning from Overrides:
  • System tracks disagreements for quality improvement
  • Helps identify edge cases for model refinement
  • Provides feedback loop for continuous learning

ScanGuru augments radiologist performance - the final clinical decision always rests with the physician.
What pathologies does ScanGuru detect?
▼
50+ pathologies live across 5 modalities (400+ on the roadmap across 17):

Chest X-Ray (examples):
  • Pulmonary: Pneumonia, nodules, masses, infiltrates, consolidation
  • Pleural: Effusion, pneumothorax, thickening
  • Cardiac: Cardiomegaly, vascular congestion
  • Skeletal: Fractures, abnormalities

MRI Spine (examples):
  • Disc herniation, degenerative disc disease
  • Spinal stenosis (central and foraminal)
  • Compression fractures, spondylolisthesis
  • Spinal cord signal abnormalities

CT, MRI Brain, Mammography, and Other Modalities: See individual modality pages for complete pathology lists.
Does ScanGuru provide differential diagnoses?
▼
ScanGuru identifies imaging findings rather than providing diagnoses.

What You Get:
  • Specific anatomical findings (e.g., "3cm nodule in right upper lobe")
  • Confidence scores for each finding
  • Location markers and measurements
  • Grad-CAM visualizations highlighting regions of interest
  • Comparison to prior studies (when available)

Clinical Context: The radiologist integrates AI findings with clinical history, patient demographics, and prior imaging to formulate differential diagnoses and recommendations.

🔒 Security & Compliance

Is ScanGuru HIPAA compliant?
▼
ScanGuru is built to align with HIPAA, GDPR, and DPDP principles — with encryption, access controls, and audit logging. There is no formal “HIPAA certification” to hold; for deployments involving protected health information we sign Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with covered entities. See our Security page for details.

Security Measures:
  • Encryption: AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit
  • Access Control: Role-based permissions, MFA, SSO support
  • Audit Logging: Complete audit trails of all system access
  • Data Isolation: Dedicated infrastructure per customer

Compliance status:
  • We are not currently SOC 2, HIPAA, ISO 13485, or HITRUST certified
  • No Business Associate Agreement (BAA) is offered today, and no audits are completed
  • Privacy designed around HIPAA, GDPR and India’s DPDP principles

We’re pursuing the appropriate frameworks as we approach pilots. See our Security page for current posture.
